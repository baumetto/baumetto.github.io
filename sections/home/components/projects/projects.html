Released products

This section displays the projects I worked on and have been publicly disclosed:

  • (2024) STM32N6 with Neural-ART accelerator™

    The STM32N6 is the first STM32 microcontroller to embed the Neural-ART accelerator™, an in-house developed Neural Processing Unit (NPU) engineered for power-efficient Edge AI applications. From 2021 to 2024 I was responsible for the verification of the core Convolution Accelerator IP embedded in the Neural-ART™ and I also contributed to the design of some of its features.

  • (2024) Audi Q6 e-tron's Digital OLED Rear Lights

    The Audi Q6 e-tron is the world-first car equipped with 60-segments digital OLED light panels. From 2019 to 2021 I was responsible for the development of the rear lights electronic system.

European projects

This section displays the public EU-funded projects I contributed to:

  • (Ongoing) NeuroSoC

    The overarching objective of NeuroSoC is to develop a flexible computing system where an analog IMC-based Neural Processing Unit (IMNPU) is integrated into a functional safe and secure Multi-Processor System-on-Chip (MPSoC) to tackle the requirements of a wide set of edge-AI applications. From 2025 I contribute to the outcome of Work Package 3 (Architecture), specifically I am responsible for the development of the core In-Memory Computing (IMC) Convolution Accelerator IP embedded in the MPSoC.
